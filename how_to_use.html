How to Use the Pet Breed Predictor

Step 1: Upload an Image

Click on the designated area or drag and drop an image of your pet onto the screen from your local storage or from the given examples..

Step 2: Wait for Prediction

The system will analyze the image using advanced algorithms to predict the breed of your pet.

Step 3: View Results

Once the analysis is complete, the predicted pet breed will be displayed along with an image for reference.

Tips:

  • Ensure the image is clear and centered on your pet's face for accurate predictions.
  • Supported image formats include JPEG, JPG, PNG, and WEBP.

That's it! Discover the breed of your beloved pet effortlessly with our Pet Breed Predictor.
